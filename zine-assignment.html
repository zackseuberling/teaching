Assignment 2, Publishing a digital Zine

Part 1 - Research

Brainstorm and research what your zine will be about. What makes a compelling choice for your zine? Choose at least 3 different topics to explore before making a final decision. It is important for me to see you have done some amount of research before choosing a topic.

You will be turning your research in along with your design and website so produce these materials in a way that can be shared (Figma, Figjam, text, etc.)

Resources

  • What is a zine?

Part 2 - Design

Design your website in Figma. We'll be writing and creating all the content for the website, so if you need to produce images, do so however you please: Photoshop, drawing and scanning documents, etc. The final design document will be a Figma file with your text and images in place.

Show your design progress. I want to see where you started and where you end up!

Part 3 - Development

Putting it all together! Using everything from class, build your website!

Requirements

  • Your HTML and CSS should pass validation.
  • Your website should be responsive to different browser sizes.
  • Your website should have at least 4 pages.
